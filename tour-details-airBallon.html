Hot Air Balloon Ride – Luxor

Soar above the Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, and the Nile River at sunrise.

45–60 minutes flight Luxor, Egypt

A Journey Above History

Start your day before sunrise as we pick you up from your hotel. Cross the Nile by boat and witness the magical sight of balloons being inflated in the dark sky. As the sun rises, drift above the timeless wonders of Luxor: the Valley of the Kings, Temple of Hatshepsut, the mighty Nile, and endless golden mountains. A safe and unforgettable adventure for every traveler.
