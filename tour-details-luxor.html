Luxor Tour

Discover the wonders of Luxor, Egypt’s greatest open-air museum

6–8 hours Luxor, Egypt

The Spirit of Luxor

Luxor is the heart of ancient Egypt, home to some of the world’s most remarkable temples and tombs. This full-day journey takes you across both the East and West Banks of the Nile, where history, mythology, and grandeur come alive.

Karnak & Luxor Temples

Your Journey Unfolds

01

Arrival & Transfer

Start your day in Luxor, often called the world’s greatest open-air museum, home to Egypt’s most impressive ancient monuments.

02

Karnak Temple

Explore the largest religious complex ever built, famous for its massive Hypostyle Hall with 134 towering columns.

03

Luxor Temple

Visit the magnificent Luxor Temple, once connected to Karnak Temple by the Avenue of Sphinxes.

04

Valley of the Kings

Step inside the tombs of the great Pharaohs, including the famous tomb of Tutankhamun.

05

Temple of Hatshepsut

Admire the unique mortuary temple of Queen Hatshepsut, beautifully carved into the cliffs of Deir el-Bahari.

06

Colossi of Memnon

Marvel at the two giant statues of Pharaoh Amenhotep III, standing tall for more than 3,000 years.

07

Lunch

Enjoy a traditional Egyptian lunch at a local restaurant overlooking the Nile.

08

Shopping

Browse local bazaars for souvenirs such as papyrus, alabaster statues, and pharaonic handicrafts.

09

Duration

Approximately 6–8 hours.
