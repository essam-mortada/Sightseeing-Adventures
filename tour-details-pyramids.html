Giza Pyramids Tour

Discover the Great Pyramids, Sphinx & Valley Temple in this unforgettable journey

4–5 hours Giza, Egypt

The Essence of Giza

Step into the land of pharaohs and marvel at the architectural genius of the ancient Egyptians. The Giza Plateau holds timeless wonders — the Great Pyramids, the enigmatic Sphinx, and sacred temples. Experience Egypt’s rich history while enjoying modern comforts like private transport, local cuisine, and authentic cultural experiences.

Your Journey Unfolds

01

Pick-up & Transfer

Your day begins with a pick-up from your hotel and a comfortable drive to the Giza Plateau.

02

The Great Pyramids

Explore the Great Pyramid of Khufu, visit the Pyramid of Khafre, and see the smaller Pyramid of Menkaure.

03

Panoramic Viewpoint

Head to the panoramic spot for breathtaking views of all three pyramids together — perfect for unforgettable photos.

04

Camel or Horse Ride

Enjoy a traditional camel or horse ride around the pyramids, just like the ancients once did.

05

The Great Sphinx

Discover the iconic Sphinx, with the body of a lion and the head of a Pharaoh, guarding the Giza Plateau.

06

Valley Temple of Khafre

Learn about ancient rituals and mummification secrets in this fascinating temple next to the Sphinx.

07

Lunch (Optional)

Stop at a local restaurant overlooking the pyramids to taste authentic Egyptian cuisine.

08

Shopping

Visit bazaars for souvenirs such as papyrus, perfumes, or miniature pharaonic statues.

09

Duration

Approximately 4–5 hours.
