Aswan Tour

Discover temples, islands, Nubian culture & Nile beauty in a full-day experience

6–7 hours Aswan, Egypt

The Essence of Aswan

Aswan is Egypt’s serene jewel on the Nile, where history, culture, and natural beauty blend together. From ancient temples to lush islands and vibrant Nubian traditions, this tour offers a unique glimpse into Egypt’s southern charm.

Your Journey Unfolds

01

Arrival & Transfer

Begin your day in Aswan, one of Egypt’s most charming cities, famous for its stunning Nile views.

02

Philae Temple

Take a boat ride across the Nile to visit the magnificent Temple of Philae, dedicated to the goddess Isis.

03

High Dam

Discover one of Egypt’s greatest engineering achievements, built to control Nile floods and generate electricity.

04

Botanical Island

Enjoy a relaxing boat trip to the lush Botanical Garden, home to rare trees and exotic plants.

05

Nubian Village

Sail on a traditional felucca to the colorful Nubian Village, where you’ll experience Nubian culture and hospitality.

06

Shopping

Explore Aswan’s lively tourist market, famous for spices, perfumes, handicrafts, and souvenirs.

07

Lunch

Enjoy a delicious meal at a local restaurant overlooking the Nile, with options of Egyptian or Nubian cuisine.

08

Duration

Approximately 6–7 hours.
