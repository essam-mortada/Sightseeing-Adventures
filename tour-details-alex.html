The Spirit of Alexandria

Where the Mediterranean breeze meets ancient history, Alexandria offers a unique blend of culture, heritage, and coastal charm. Once the shining jewel of the ancient world, this city still carries the essence of knowledge, art, and timeless beauty. From its legendary library to its serene seafront, Alexandria is a destination that captures both heart and imagination.

Bibliotheca Alexandrina

Visit the modern Library of Alexandria, one of the most important cultural centers in the world, housing millions of books, museums, and exhibition halls.

02

Qaitbay Citadel

Explore the 15th-century fortress built on the site of the legendary Lighthouse of Alexandria, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

03

Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa

Discover the fascinating underground Roman tombs, considered one of the most remarkable archaeological sites in Egypt.

Roman Amphitheatre
